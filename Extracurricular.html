My Extracurriculars & Leadership Experience

Here are a collection of organization, and leadership roles I have taken from my junior highschool leading up to the most recent.


Ramon Magsaysay Highschool Chorale | 2021 - 2023

This was my only organization throughout my junior highschool journey, where I took the chance to audition despite being really afraid of performing infront of other people. Here I met so many new people, and learned how to be a better member in a group. I also took on a leadership role at my time at the choir, which was section leader where I led the practice with my fellow sopranos.


Silab Agnayan | 2024 - 2025

This is one of the organizations that I joined during my senior highschool year. Previously known as MaSci, it is now called Silab Agnayan. Here I was one of those in charge of multimedia arts, where I edited and designed posts for the official facebook page, as well as the booth that we created during the intramurals week, to add I was also one of the external officers in charge with communicating with the other strand organizations as well as the supreme student council.


Intramurals Esports Committee | 2024 - 2025

This is one of the committees I joined, here it was created in order to handle the esports category in the intramurals week. Here, we were in charge of spectating, facilitating the ongoing games, as well as tallying the scores to determine the winners. We were also equipped with first aid skills and kit in case of any medical emergencies.
